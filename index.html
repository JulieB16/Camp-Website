About Us

This camp is run by Julie Belfor who has coached for the Cincinnati United Juniors Program, which consists of ages 2 - 9. Combining her passion for soccer and community service, Julie created this camp to provide a fun and inclusive environment for young players to build skills and confidence. All proceeds go to Soccer Without Borders, a nonprofit organization that empowers underprivileged youth around the world through the game of soccer.
